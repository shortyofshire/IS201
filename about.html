About Me

Who I am and what I like to do

My name is Audrey Hill. I am the youngest of 4. I was born in Denver, Colorado in 2003. I have lived in a couple places, but since 2012 I have been living in a small rural town in Oregon called Cove. Cove has a population of 500, that's how small it is.

Last year I was lucky enough to be accepted into Brigham Young University in Provo, Utah. Since then, I have been attending college and working hard to achieve my goals. I am currently working towards a degree in Accounting.

I love to travel, especially with my family. I particularly like to spend my time in theme parks such as Disney or Universal. If I'm not out adventuring, you can find me watching the latest movies or playing some sort of crazy video game with my brother Bailey.
